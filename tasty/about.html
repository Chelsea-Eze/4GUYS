About our Restaurant

The Restaurant

In the heart of Abuja, 4GUYS is not just a restaurant, it is a world. It is a unique place, where every object and every detail have their own meaning, because they are elements that contribute to making a journey to discover the food and wine culture of the area. It is an opportunity to have an intense and enveloping taste experience, in which aromas, colors, flavors, combined with a spark of inspiration and creativity, ignite all the senses. It is a place to come back to meet with friends, because those who come to visit us are not just a customer, they are a person to welcome, to accompany, to greet with a goodbye.

Delve into the 4GUYS dining experience. Join today’s top chefs for interactive cooking opportunities, high-profile dinners and other culinary experiences. Get to know the chefs, mixologists and sommeliers in an intimate beachfront setting.
